Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

St. Xavier's High School, Fort, is a private Catholic primary and secondary school for boys located in Fort, Mumbai, India. The English medium school was founded in 1869 and is run by the Society of Jesus.



School Library

Was built in 1980

The college facilitates a well-stocked library with collection of more than eighteen thousand books on different subjects. International and National Journals and Magazines from various disciplines are also available.

School Achievements

Inter School Debate On Enviroment Pollution
Won first prize in inter school debate on enviroment pollution

The team of Auckland House School, Shimla, was adjudged the winner and International Sahaja School, Dharamsala, was chosen as a runners-up. In the best speakers category,Pranav Rawal from Auckland House was adjudged winner and Varshi Negi runners-up.

Inter School Football Competition
Came second in inter school football competition

The school competes at inter-school and taluka and state-level football competitions. On 17 December 2015, St. Xavier's High School lost to Rosary High School, Navelim by a 2-1 defeat in the All Goa Inter-School Under 17 Invitational Football Tournament at Chinchinim Church ground. The tournament was held in remembrance and honour of St. Joseph Vaz and was organised by Mount Mary's School and Higher Secondary School.

School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

Extensively used by the students for extra-curricular activities and for hosting functions of the College, SRCC auditorium has a seating capacity of about 750 people. It has an advanced light and sound system that is managed by specially designated support staff along with the students. It is equipped with a large stage, one green rooms, wall paneling for acoustic effects, air conditioning and podium.
